Our retreat days are inspired by our commitment to working with Mother Nature and the enchanted landscapes of South Wales in which we find ourselves. In both our farming practices, family life and personal ethos we work in harmony with nature. We feel a calling to share what we have and welcome people of all backgrounds to lose themselves in the magic of the rolling Welsh hills, locally sourced food and drink and deep spiritual relaxation. Mindfulness, sound healing, yoga and art are just some of the therapeutic practices you can look forward to on our retreat days.

Our stone circle is at the heart of our retreat day activities. Built several years ago by local experts using original stone from the 16th century farm, the circle has been mapped and aligned with both the solstices, equinoxes and our family birthdays; making it a sacred space where we love to welcome guests to relax and embark upon meditative practices.

Nant Y Gaseg Farm is an undiscovered gem in the rural landscape and the perfect setting for your once-in-a-lifetime occasion. Tucked away in the Swansea Valley, just fifteen miles from the city centre, the farm is the perfect location for an exclusive wedding day like no other.

Our traditionally cultivated farm can offer no end of picturesque wedding moments to capture; from sunflower fields and orchards to our stone circle and wildflower fields! We can create a day that is truly your own.

Find Out More

Have fun and lets get imaginative in creating your wedding! We love nothing more than supporting your ideas and helping you find a package that's right for you. Food trucks, ice cream vans, grazing tables... the possibilites to cater for you big day are endless!

The canvas stretch tent from Afon Events has a capacity of up to 80 people and offers the perfect place to dine, drink and dance late into the night with your nearest and dearest. Let us help you find local and passionate florists, stylists and hire companies that will work with your vision and decorate the space for you.

When the night is over you and your guests can retire to The Old Barn, a 17th-century, cosy barn conversion right here on the farm! Looking for more accomodation? Our 4 person bell tents are the perfect way to end the day under canvas.

Visit the farm and find out more about our intimate weekend packages full of celebrations with your loved ones, in the tranquility of thirty acres of private Welsh landscape. Why not extend your big occasion beyond just one day and stay with us for the weekend!
